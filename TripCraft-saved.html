Singapore

A 3-Day curated experience in January

3 Days • January • Urban Discovery
Day 1

Marina Bay Exploration

Start your journey with the city's iconic mascot at Merlion Park. Cross the Jubilee Bridge toward the Esplanade for harbor views before diving into the lush Cloud Forest at Gardens by the Bay. End your night with the Garden Rhapsody light show and the panoramic heights of the Marina Bay Sands SkyPark.

Must-See Night Life
Day 2

Cultural Heritage & Local Flavors

Immerse yourself in history at the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple, followed by a culinary adventure in Chinatown. Discover the vibrant shophouses and temples of Little India, then finish in Kampong Glam, where the golden-domed Sultan Mosque stands amidst the trendy, mural-clad walls of Haji Lane.

Dining Tip Photo Spot
Day 3

Nature & Leisure

Reconnect with nature at the UNESCO-listed Singapore Botanic Gardens. Spend the afternoon exploring Sentosa Island’s aquatic wonders or relaxing on the sands of Tanjong Beach. Conclude your Singapore escapade with a golden-hour river cruise at Clarke Quay followed by an elegant riverside dining experience.

Relaxation Sunset
