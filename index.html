Journey Through Time

Xi'an, China

10 Days in July • Cultural Immersion

Region Focus

City Center • Qujiang • Lintong

Duration

10 Days / 10 Nights

Explore Schedule
Days 1-2: City Center

Ancient Fortifications

Arrive at the heart of the city. Pedal along the ancient 14km City Wall at sunrise, followed by exploring the vibrant Muslim Quarter for authentic mutton soup and spicy cold rice noodles.

Days 3-4: The Tang Legacy

Dynastic Splendor

Immerse yourself in Tang Dynasty art at the Shaanxi History Museum. Transition to the Qujiang district to marvel at the Giant Wild Goose Pagoda and the modern lights of the Great Tang All Day Mall.

Days 5-6: Serenity & Art

Gardens and Culture

Relax by the lake at Tang Paradise and explore the serene courtyards of the Small Wild Goose Pagoda before diving into the creative energy of the Textile City Art District.

Days 7-8: Lintong District

Archaeological Marvels

Witness the silent, disciplined rows of the Terracotta Army. Spend the evening at Huaqing Palace for the spectacular outdoor theater performance, 'Song of Everlasting Sorrow'.

Must-See Cultural Highlight
Days 9-10: Peak & Palate

Heights and Flavors

Conquer the granite peaks of Mount Hua by cable car for ethereal views. On your final morning, savor authentic, hand-pulled Biang Biang noodles at a local market before heading home.
